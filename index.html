🧠 Reflection: Mock Interview Video (STAR Method)

Situation:In order to prepare for the job market, I showcased a recording of a two-minute interview video to showcase my communication skills as well as my adherence to professional standards in the industry.

Task:To provide well-thought-out answers to frequently asked questions during interviews, conduct myself in a professional manner on camera and make sure that the final video meets the technical specifications for the digital portfolio.

Action:I practiced the speech in order to deliver it within the required two minutes, made sure that my surroundings were quiet when recording the video. The speech included clear pronunciation and professional body language.

Result: I was able to successfully record and embed an interview video lasting for two minutes into my digital portfolio. This made me more confident about using cameras and helped me learn how to pitch myself in less than two minutes.
